OUR STORY

Built from the inside out.

We know this industry because we built our business in it.

"We spent fifteen years learning what Indian grocery and food businesses actually need. Karyaa is what we built when we realised we could share that knowledge with the world."

— Founding Team, Siddab Ventures Pvt Ltd

Karyaa was not born in a boardroom. It grew out of fifteen years of hands-on experience in the Indian grocery export industry through our parent company, MESHA Exports. We have loaded containers, resolved customs queries, managed supplier relationships, and served hundreds of importers across the UK, USA, Canada, Australia, and New Zealand.

That experience taught us something most staffing companies never learn: this industry has its own language, its own rhythms, and its own complexity. A generic virtual assistant — however talented — simply cannot replace someone who already understands what a FSSAI certificate is, why a container's country of origin matters at UK customs, or how Indian grocery pricing cycles work.

Karyaa exists to give Indian grocery and food businesses the dedicated, knowledgeable operations support they deserve — people who show up every day knowing your business, not learning it. We recruit specifically for this sector, train within our own ecosystem, and stand behind every placement with a five-day replacement guarantee.
